🔍 What is Private DNS?

Private DNS (specifically DNS-over-HTTPS) is a protocol that encrypts your website requests, making them unreadable by your ISP and trackers. Instead of using the default, insecure resolver from your internet provider, you use a secure endpoint that you control.

🚀 Why Use It?

🛡️ Ad Blocking

Blocks ads and malicious scripts at the source, before they reach your browser.

👁️ Total Privacy

Protects your browsing history from being monetized by third parties.

⚡ Performance

Optimized resolver speeds reduce latency and make the web feel faster.

The most effective way to use Shield DNS is to configure it directly in your browser. This provides encrypted, high-performance lookups on all platforms.
